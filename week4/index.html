AmazingShe

Reaching out for the advancement of adolescent girls

Mission:

  • To inspire the female youth to grow into beautiful young ladies inside and out.
  • To motivate and engage in their lives, showing them how to obtain a healthy relationship within themselves, family, and friends.
  • To encourage them to make better decisions in life, preparing them for the real world and giving them real-life hands on experience.

Focus:

We will primarily target:

  • Preteens, ages 10-12
  • Teens, ages 13-19
